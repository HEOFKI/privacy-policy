Data Deletion Instructions for Jetmav

To delete your data from the Jetmav app, please follow these steps:

Open the Jetmav app on your device.

Tap on the “More” tab (usually located at the bottom right).

Select “Delete Account” from the menu.

Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Once confirmed, your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted from our servers in compliance with Facebook’s data policy.

If you need further assistance, please contact us at: info@jetmav.com
